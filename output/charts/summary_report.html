💡 Key Insights

Why Random Forest Wins

  • Detects 3 more fraud cases (6 vs 3) - critical for security
  • Lower false negative rate (2 vs 5) - fewer missed frauds
  • Higher recall score - better at catching actual fraud
  • Handles non-linear patterns better
  • More robust to outliers and noise

Performance Metrics Explained

  • Accuracy: Overall correctness (% of right predictions)
  • Precision: Of fraud alerts, how many were correct
  • Recall: Of actual fraud, how many were caught
  • F1-Score: Balance between precision and recall
